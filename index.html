LABEL INFO

Mint Tea is a record label based in Bristol, UK run by owner Henry James.

The label launched about two years ago and has had 4 releases to date.

The label aims to evolve alongside the house and techno scene, taking snapshots from different perspectives by offering a platform for emerging artists to release their work and put their stamp on the scene.
